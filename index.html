Pupgrade U ™ is a web application designed to match its users with an adoptable shelter dog. Our nonsensically meaningful questionnaire collects data about you and your lifestyle. That data combined with a little behind the scenes magic renders a list of adorable, loving dogs waiting to be your new family member.

Below, you'll be prompted to enter your zip code and then given the options of "Find me a pup" and "Pupgrade Me". "Find me a pup" will direct you to our quiz, while "Pupgrade Me" will allow you to browse furry friends on your own.




Our name is inspired by Beyoncé's "Upgrade U", released in 2006 on her sophomore album, B'Day. We feel Beyoncé is a good enough reason for anything.
